Medical Device Registration Electronic Submission Workshop
1 source
This source is an excerpt from the Taiwan Food and Drug Administration (TFDA) detailing a workshop about the Medical Device Registration Electronic Submission System. The documentation outlines the workflow and regulations for the electronic submission system, which is intended for medical device businesses applying for registration, modification, or extension of Class II and III medical device licenses. Key information covered includes how to log in using an IC card, the options for online or offline application, and the various payment methods available. The material also touches on future plans to expand the electronic submission service to include Class I medical devices and emphasizes the goal of improving efficiency through digitalization.

電子化送件申請常見QA (Common Q&A for Electronic Submission Application)
送審封面 (Submission Cover Sheet) 下載送審文件封面並置於郵寄資料首頁，無封面者食品藥物管理署將不受理。 (Download the submission document cover sheet and place it on the first page of the mailed documents; submissions without a cover sheet will not be accepted by the FDA.)
資料填寫 (Data Entry) 請於【資料說明】簡述上傳文件內容(有助於加速審查)，相同資料(檔案)請不要重複上傳。 (Please briefly describe the content of the uploaded documents in [Data Description] (this helps speed up the review); do not upload identical data/files repeatedly.)
補件作業 (Supplementary Submission Process)
• 補件/申復： 透過系統上傳補件資料，建議下載備份檔，並連同正本文件（若適用）及補件/申復封面於送件次日起3個工作天內郵寄至食品藥物管理署。無封面者不受理。 (Supplement/Appeal: Upload supplementary documents through the system, it is recommended to download a backup file, and include the original hardcopy documents (if applicable) and the supplement/appeal cover sheet, mailing them to the Food and Drug Administration within 3 working days from the submission date. Submissions without a cover sheet will not be accepted.)
• 撤案： 於系統提交撤案申請，並書面行文食品藥物管理署（註明系統流水號及撤案理由）。 (Case Withdrawal: Submit the withdrawal application in the system, and send a formal letter to the Food and Drug Administration (stating the system flow number and reason for withdrawal).)
近期更新規定(1) (Recent Updates (1))
• 查驗登記申請案： 標籤、說明書或包裝擬稿可於系統檢附掃描檔，無須先寄出正本(待領證時再檢附標籤、說明書或包裝擬稿二份) (Registration application cases: Drafts of labels, instructions, or packaging can be submitted as scanned files in the system, and the originals do not need to be mailed first (originals must be provided in duplicate when collecting the license))
• 變更申請案： 應連同原許可證正本、原核准並蓋有中央主管機關核定章之標籤、說明書或包裝正本(若適用)、標籤、說明書或包裝擬稿二份(若適用)等連同送審文件封面一併寄出。 (Change application cases: Must be mailed together with the original license certificate, the original approved and stamped label/instruction/packaging (if applicable), and two copies of the proposed label/instruction/packaging drafts (if applicable), along with the submission document cover sheet.)
近期更新規定(2) (Recent Updates (2))
• 自114年7月1日起，部分類別(第二、三等級)申請案全面使用電子化送件(不再接受紙本送件*)： 臨床前測試資料替代之查登、變更案、專供外銷、同一產品不同品名申請案。 (Starting July 1, 2025, certain categories (Class II and III) of applications must use electronic submission exclusively (hardcopy submissions will no longer be accepted*): registration and change cases using preclinical test data alternatives, cases exclusively for export, and applications for the same product under a different name.)
• 自114年7月1日起，免另外檢附補件公文(無其他紙(正)本文件應檢附時適用)。 (Starting July 1, 2025, supplementary submissions do not require the attachment of official letters (applicable when no other physical original documents are required).)
• **若有特殊情形經食品藥物管理署同意得以紙本送件之申請商，請逐案檢附同意函影本，同意函之效期為3年。應以電子化送件申請，卻以紙本送件且未檢附前述同意函者，其查驗登記申請案無法通過形式審查，變更案則予退件退費。 (*If an applicant is permitted by the Food and Drug Administration to submit in hardcopy due to special circumstances, a photocopy of the consent letter must be attached for each case; the consent letter is valid for 3 years. If electronic submission is required but hardcopy is used without the consent letter, the registration application will fail formal review, and the change application will be rejected and refunded.)
